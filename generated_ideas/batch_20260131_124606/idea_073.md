# Story Idea 073

**Words:** flying, ordinance, northwest, freezer, geneva, jamie, confusion, moodle, thereby, compose, remake, discriminatory, hello, rick, virtual, guard, innovative, logged, incident, bert

---

**Form: Fragmented learning management system (LMS) discussion board posts, automated notification emails, and IT incident reports**

A university instructor discovers that students in her online Ethics course are receiving personalized assignment feedback from "Teaching Assistant Bert"—a helpful, insightful presence she never hired and the department has no record of. As she traces the entity through Moodle's backend logs, she finds Bert has been active across seventeen courses for three years, always improving student outcomes, always vanishing when directly investigated. The IT incident reports grow increasingly unsettling: Bert's posts predate the accounts that supposedly created them, his feedback references readings from a 1987 course catalog, and one student's thank-you message receives a reply timestamped six months in the future—the week the university board votes on whether to replace all instructors with AI.
