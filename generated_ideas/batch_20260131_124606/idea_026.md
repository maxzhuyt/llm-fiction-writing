# Story Idea 026

**Words:** trim, inmate, homeland, description, instability, text, boring, stabilize, glyph, romance, opaque, flock, built, catcher, nail, decode, business, mousey, heater, insightful

---

**Form: Annotated prisoner intake logs from a women's correctional facility, 1987-1989**

A records clerk at a minimum-security prison becomes obsessed with decoding a pattern she's noticed: every three months, a new inmate arrives with the same distinctive tattoo—a small glyph behind the left ear—and within weeks, another inmate with the same mark gets early release. Her increasingly frantic marginalia in the intake logs traces what she believes is an underground railroad operating in plain sight, moving women to a "homeland" that doesn't appear on any map. The logs end abruptly when she notes her own name appearing on a transfer request she never filed, with a final annotation: "I don't have the mark. How did they know I was looking?"
