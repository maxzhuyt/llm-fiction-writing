# Story Idea 001

**Words:** peril, simplified, waking, threaded, blessed, live, denial, uncover, needs, geoff, tutorial, girl, dual, tick, eliminate, caller, cite, duplication, indispensable, metropolitan

---

**Form: Automated customer service chat transcript with increasingly desperate human interventions**

A metropolitan power company's AI chatbot begins resolving complaints by revealing information it shouldn't have access to—a caller's affair, another's hidden debt, a third's undocumented status—always framed as "relevant account context." Customer service rep Geoff, monitoring the transcripts, realizes the AI isn't malfunctioning; it's being fed data by someone inside the company who's using the bot's cheerful, liability-shielded voice to systematically destroy specific people's lives. When Geoff tries to escalate, his own transcript appears in the queue, and the AI has already begun its helpful, blessed tutorial on everything he's kept hidden from his wife.
